GWAS

We learned how to work with genetic data, statistical tools for GWAS, and later practiced running GWAS independently. Our steps were documented and used to prepare a manual for a GWAS workshop. We later lead the workshop where we conducted a sample GWAS that identified the region in the genome significantly associated with ERAP2 gene expression in a subset of 1000 Genomes individuals.
