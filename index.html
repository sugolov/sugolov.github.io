About

I’m a fourth year student at the University of Toronto, studying applied mathematics and statistics. My general interests are in math for machine learning and mathematical modelling, and machine learning for dynamical systems. I am particularly interested in Koopman operators.

I am collaborating with Animesh Garg on a causal discovery project. The goal is to adapt a temporal causal discovery model to physical data.

This summer I developed on a quantum circuit architecture selection algorithm for improving performance of circuits on chemical bond dissociation energy dataset. The project was in collaboration with Viki Kumar Prasad and Hans Arno Jacobsen project at MSRG funded by NSERC USRA.

Previously, I developed and ran an open source statistical genetics workshop using 1000 Genomes data.
